HITS COLLEGE

Helapuri Institute Of Technology And Science Eluru also known as HITS ELURU, is a Private College, affiliated to JNTU KAKINADA situated in Eluru, Andhra Pradesh. HITS ELURU was established in the year 2009. HITS ELURU is approved by AICTE. HITS ELURU offers admission in various courses like B.Tech, M.Tech.
